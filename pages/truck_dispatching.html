Your Learning Blueprint

A comprehensive course prepares you for the real-world demands of dispatching. The curriculum is a journey that builds your skills step-by-step, from industry basics to expert-level negotiation.

Introduction to the Trucking Industry

Learn the key players, equipment types, and essential terminology to speak the language of logistics.

Regulations and Compliance

Master the rules of the road, including Hours of Service (HOS) and electronic logging devices (ELDs).

Dispatching Tools and Technology

Get hands-on training with industry-standard software like load boards, GPS, and routing systems.

Load Hunting and Booking

Develop strategies to find and secure the most profitable loads for your drivers or clients.

Negotiation Skills

Learn techniques to effectively negotiate with freight brokers to maximize rates and profitability.

Administrative & Financial Management

Understand the paperwork, invoicing, and financial basics of running a dispatching business.

Communication and Problem-Solving

Develop the critical soft skills needed to handle any situation, from driver issues to unexpected delays.
